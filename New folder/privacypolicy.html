Privacy Policy

Welcome to BVL Cruises

At BVL Cruises, we prioritize the privacy of our customers and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you make a booking or inquiry, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We may collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, and browsing patterns through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communications: Any correspondence you have with our team, including emails and phone calls, may be recorded and stored.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Process Bookings: Your personal and payment information is used to process and confirm your cruise reservations.
  • Customer Support: We use your contact information to respond to your inquiries and provide customer service.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may send promotional emails or newsletters about offers, special deals, or news related to BVL Cruises. You can opt-out at any time.
  • Improving Our Services: We analyze user behavior to improve our website functionality and enhance your overall experience with BVL Cruises.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following instances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist in processing bookings, managing payments, and delivering services (e.g., payment processors, customer support platforms).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of BVL Cruises, our customers, or others.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and we encourage you to protect your account and information.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, understand website traffic, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for processing your data.
  • Request the transfer of your data to another service provider.
